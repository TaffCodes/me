Project Overview

FundManage is an intelligent fund management platform designed for modern investors. It provides a unified dashboard to track, analyze, and manage financial assets from various brokerage accounts in real-time. By leveraging data analytics, the platform offers deep insights into portfolio performance, risk exposure, and investment diversification, empowering users to make smarter financial decisions.

Problem Statement

Investors often hold assets across multiple platforms (e.g., different stockbrokers, mutual fund houses, crypto exchanges), making it difficult to get a holistic view of their financial health. This fragmentation leads to inefficient tracking, poor risk management, and missed opportunities. Manual consolidation is time-consuming, prone to errors, and lacks sophisticated analytical capabilities.

Solution

FundManage securely integrates with various financial institutions to automatically consolidate a user's entire portfolio into one intuitive interface. The platform features advanced analytics, performance attribution, risk assessment tools, and automated reporting. An AI-powered assistant provides personalized insights, such as identifying over-concentrated positions or suggesting opportunities for tax-loss harvesting.

Design Process

Empathize

Conducted in-depth interviews with retail investors and financial advisors to identify their primary challenges in portfolio management and reporting.

Define

The core user need was defined as achieving a clear, consolidated, and actionable overview of all investments without manual data entry.

Ideate

Brainstormed features including secure API integrations, customizable dashboards, risk analysis modules, and performance comparison benchmarks.

Prototype

Developed interactive wireframes and high-fidelity mockups focusing on an intuitive user journey for account linking and data visualization.

Test

Ran usability tests with a beta group of investors, using their feedback to refine the dashboard layout and the clarity of analytical charts.

Impact

Users reported a 75% reduction in time spent on manual portfolio tracking and spreadsheet management.

Improved financial literacy and decision-making, with a 30% increase in users rebalancing their portfolios based on platform insights.

Received highly positive feedback for the platform's ability to provide a single, reliable source of truth for their entire net worth.
